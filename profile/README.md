# Eye to Eye

**Advancing pediatric eye and vision health through AI-driven early detection and access for underserved communities.**

Eye to Eye is a student-led organization building open-source tools, conducting research, and partnering with NGOs to close the gap in pediatric eye care access worldwide.

## What we do
- 🔬 **Research** — the biology and optics behind pediatric eye conditions
- 💻 **Engineering & AI** — open-source screening and detection tools
- 🌍 **Advocacy** — fundraising and awareness for pediatric eye care access

## Open source
Our tools are released under permissive licenses so any clinic, NGO, or researcher can use and build on them.
